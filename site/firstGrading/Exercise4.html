What is football?

The most popular game all over the world; It occupies the largest number of followers and the largest number of players who practice it, according to the reports of the International Football Association, the number of players playing this game at the end of the twentieth century reached about 250 million and more than 1.3 billion followers and spectators, and football is considered a sport within the reach of everyone where it can be practiced In different places such as gyms, official stadiums, or even streets, parks, or many other places. Football is also a team sport based on the principle of cooperation and team spirit, where two teams are formed each consisting of eleven players who pass the ball between them in order to enter it into the opponent's goal, and during that time they are prohibited from using their hands to deal with the ball, except for the goalkeeper Who is allowed to do so as long as he is inside what is known as the penalty area, and the game ends with the victory of the team that scores the most goals in the opponent's goal.

the way of playing

Football is played according to specific laws known as the Laws of the Game. A ball of 71 cm in circumference is used to play and is known as "football". Two teams compete in the game, and each team must get the ball into the other team's goal and in this way score a goal. The team that scores the most goals at the end of the match wins. If both teams score the same number of goals or do not score any, then a draw occurs. Each team is led by a captain who has only one official mission as stipulated in the rules of the game, which is: Participate in the coin toss before the start of the match or penalty kicks. The basic rule in the game is that players other than the goalkeeper do not touch the ball with their hand or arm during the game unless They were throwing a throw-in. Although players usually use their feet to move the ball, they can use any other part of their body except their hand or arm. During play, players can kick the ball in any direction and move freely on the field as long as the ball is not played for a player standing offside.

Main elements of the game

  • Ball:It is the most important element in the game, as many international companies manufacture the ball in which football is played, with advanced, modern designs and graded since the date of its initial industry, which dates back to nearly a hundred years ago; The ball, which consisted of 32 pieces of leather, was manufactured until it developed in our present time and became composed of only 8 pieces.
  • The stadium: The stadium: It is the place where the two teams play, and the average length of the stadium on which the official football matches take place is approximately 100 meters, while the average width is 60 meters.
  • Goal:The goal for each team is placed on the center of the goal line, and for the goal are two side lists, each of which is placed at an equal distance from the corner signal, and these two columns are connected from the top by a horizontal beam, and these two columns in addition to the crossbar must be made on A circular, square, rectangular, or oval shape, but on condition that it does not pose a danger to the players in any way.

Football History

Football has a very ancient history dating back to the second and third centuries AD. Where it was known in China as "Tzu Shu" as its first start in human life; As the evidence indicates that it was a military training at the time; Where the player was trying to enter the ball into the net fixed on bamboo sticks, and the ball they were playing with was stuffed with hair and feathers, and football also appeared in Japan as "Kirami", which remained until now played in its original form in Japanese culture, and this game was played in episodes A circle inside which players gather and pass the ball between them without touching the ground, and in 1300 AD football appeared in England; The players were playing with a ball made from the animal bladder, and a ban was imposed on those who played football; Given the fear of the ruling authority at the time that people would be busy with it instead of learning how to shoot; This is because England was in the midst of its war with Scotland at the time. The spread of Duqm football continued until 1904 when the presence of an international official body concerned with the affairs of this game became necessary. So the International Football Association(FIFA) was established.
