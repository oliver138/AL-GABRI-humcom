Five steps to making a home garden


Creating a home garden is not impossible or difficult, but you need to follow some steps when starting to prepare.

Steps to make a home garden:

  • Determining the type of garden:(Is it a home garden to grow vegetables and fruits?,Or a garden to give a wonderful appearance to the house?,
    Or a garden to grow plants, aromatic herbs and flowers?) So, the type of garden must be determined first.

  • Choosing the appropriate design for the home garden from different designs according to taste and garden area.

  • Determine the type of plants that will be grown in the garden, taking care to choose the plants that grow naturally in that area.

  • Choosing the right place for cultivation in terms of good soil and availability of sun, water and natural air.

  • Irrigation of the soil and application of fertilizers necessary for the normal and healthy growth of plants.

Tools and materials you may need:

  1. Seeds

  2. Fertilizers

  3. Water hose

  4. Cultivation tools

  5. Agricultural seedlings
